human centered design + rapid prototyping
project // wooden kinoma create
occasion/context
prototype engineering internship at Kinoma
user group
Kinoma Create users, makers
goal
I wanted to show customers that the appearance of the Kinoma Create is customizable, provide a basic blueprint for laser cutting an enclosure and outline a way to safely transplant the electronics into a new enclosure.
notes
During my process, I damaged several ribbon cables, causing the touch screens on units to malfunction. I consulted with electronics experts to diagnose the issues and kept track of best practices to protect the electronics during the assembly.
testing
After arriving at a case + instructions, I observed 3 members of the team build the case to make sure that safe results were reproduced and made revisions to meet their confusions.
outcome
I ended up with a 3-piece wooden case, assembled with flat head screws, nuts and rubber grommets.
